Parmesan Garlic Ramen

Description

What can I say? It's ramen but trying really hard to be fancy... Coders can't always be picky, ok?

Ingredients

  • 3 packs of instant ramen
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried italian seasoning
