Parmesan Garlic Ramen

Description

What can I say? It's ramen but trying really hard to be fancy... Coders can't always be picky, ok?

Ingredients

  • 3 packs of instant ramen
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil 6 cups of water, add noodles and boil for 3 minutes
  2. Drain, save 1 cup of noodle water and set aside
  3. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for about 1 minute
  4. Stir in parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and noodle water. Cook until cheese is melted and sauce is thick
  5. Add noodles and toss gently until heated through. Top with parsley if desired and serve
