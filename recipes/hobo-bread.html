Hobo Bread

Description

Don't be fooled by the name, this bread is great for everyone! Regardless of housing status.

Ingredients

  • Raisins
  • Boiling water
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Pecans and/or walnuts
  • Powdered sugar glaze (if desired)

Steps

  1. Boil water, remove from heat, add raisins and let sit for one hour. Do not pour off extra water.
  2. Whisk together flour, sugar, salt, baking soda in large bowl. Pour in soaked raisins with extra water, vanila extract,and butter. Mix with spoon
  3. Fold in nuts.
  4. Spray a 9 x 5 loaf pan with cooking spray. Spread dough in pan. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 45-50 minutes or until centre tests done.
  5. Remove from oven and cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing. Add powedered sugar glaze if desired.
