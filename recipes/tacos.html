Tacos!

Description

A classic American staple. From Mexico! Who doesn't love tacos on a Tuesday? Or Thursday? Or really, any day is a good day to be Taco Day!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Taco shells (hard or soft)
  • Mixed shredded cheeses
  • Salsa
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Add beef and a small amount of water to pan on high heat
  2. Mix in taco seasoning as beef cooks. Fry beef until there is no pink visible in any part
  3. Once fully cooked, remove beef from heat
  4. Shred cheese(s) to be used in bowl and set asside
  5. Chop or shred lettuce, chop tomatoes
  6. Put tomatoes back in the fridge
  7. Lay taco shell on plate, load with cheese, beef, salsa, sour cream, and lettuce
  8. Serve tacos and rejoice!
