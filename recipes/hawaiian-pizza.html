Hawaiian Pizza

Description

Don't be fooled by the name, this style of pizza is actually a Canadian invention!

A deliciously baked crust, a base of classic tomato sauce and shredded mozzarella, topped with sliced ham and the ever controversial chunks of pineapple that give this pizza it's tropical flavour

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1tbs oliv oil
  • 1tbs cornmeal
  • half-cup pizza sauce
  • 1 and a half cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • half-cup cooked ham, sliced or chopped
  • half-cup fresh pineapple chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475F (246C). Allow it to heat for at least 15-20 minutes.
  2. Using your fingers, push dents into the surface of the dough to prevent bubbling. Brush olive oil all over the dough
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the surface of the dough to your preference, then add cheese
  4. Finally, add ham and pineapple. I recommend adding extra pineapple to upset those who don't appreciate the beauty of a Hawaiian pizza
  5. Bake for 15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown
  6. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes before slicing and serving
